## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of S.H.I.E.L.D. very seriously. If you have any concerns that potential unauthorized access, data loss, or other security related issues might be present in our software, please do not hesitate to contact us.

### Process

1. **Do not create a public issue.**
2. Email your findings to <victor.kane@example.com>.
3. Include as much information as possible to reproduce the vulnerability (code samples, logs, screenshots).
4. We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours.
5. We will provide a timeline for the fix and release.

Thank you for helping keep S.H.I.E.L.D. safe!
